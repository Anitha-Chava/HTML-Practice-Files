About RISE Engineering College

Established in 2009 by RISE (Rural Institute of Social and Economic Empowerment),
the institution is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University,
Kakinada (JNTUK) and approved by AICTE and the Government of Andhra Pradesh

About CSE-Dept

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) was established in the year 2009 with an intake of 60 students. The department is accredited by NBA and has well qualified and experienced faculty members. The department has well equipped laboratories with the latest hardware and software to meet the academic and research needs of the students and faculty members. The department is committed to provide quality education and to prepare students to meet the challenges of the ever-evolving field of computer science and engineering.
